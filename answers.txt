
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: It makes the process of multiple
editors on one project working together easier.
Now, they can share their code without merge
conflicts and work together on the same folder.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to every
field and domain. Data visualization can help
in medicine, public policy, sports, finance, etc.
